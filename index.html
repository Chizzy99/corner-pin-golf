Blog of the Recent Tournament

The 2024 tour championship took place in the Lake District and was being played at three locations, Kirkby/Lonsdale Golf club, Caruss Green Golf club and Windermere Golf club.
Day one was at Kirkby/Lonsdale and as we arrived the weather was against us. We all took on the challenge but was halted at the 6th hole where the days play was abandoned. Day two was at our main acommondation course Caruss Green which was a awesome setting that gave all players a fair but challenging test. The final days play was at Windermere, a hard test of fitness and a solid golf game would see you in the prizes.
One player stood out amongst all the tour players.
Well done to David Tills 2024 Tour Champion!
