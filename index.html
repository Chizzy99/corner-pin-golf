Blog of the Recent Tournament

The 2024 Memorial trophy took place at North Wales golf club Llandudno. Named after the late Ian "Yozzer" Hughes the competition takes place at christmas time where many golfers come together for rememberance and celebration at the end of the year.
North Wales presents a true links test with weather conditions frequently contributing at this time of the year. For once the weather was kind with just a slight fog in the air so the scoring was strong.
One player stood out amongst the travelling golfers and a worthy winner.

Well done to Ben Hughes the 2024 Memorial Champion!

Special Mentions - To all the commitee from every member, thanks for all you do.
